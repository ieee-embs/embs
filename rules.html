EMBS Blog Community Rules

Last Updated: May 06, 2025

1. Purpose of the Community

The EMBS Blog, powered by IEEE EMBS at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, aims to foster a respectful and collaborative environment for students to share knowledge, ideas, and experiences through blog posts. These rules ensure that our community remains a safe and productive space for all members.

2. Prohibition of Hate Speech

Students are strictly prohibited from spreading hate speech, derogatory comments, or any form of harassment directed at the college, its administration, faculty, staff, or fellow students. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Discriminatory remarks based on race, gender, religion, or other protected characteristics.
  • Defamatory statements about the college or its administration.
  • Content that incites hostility or violence.

Disclaimer: IEEE EMBS and Ramaiah Institute of Technology are not responsible for any hate speech or violations committed by students. Individuals engaging in such behavior will be solely accountable for their actions, and the college reserves the right to take disciplinary action.

3. Respectful Communication

All interactions on the EMBS Blog must be respectful and constructive. Prohibited behaviors include:

  • Posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate language.
  • Engaging in bullying, harassment, or personal attacks.
  • Sharing content that is disruptive or off-topic.

4. Content Submission Standards

When submitting blogs, students must adhere to the following standards:

  • Content must be original and not plagiarized from other sources.
  • Blogs should be relevant to the college community, such as academic topics, tech innovations, or student experiences.
  • Do not include promotional or advertising content unless approved by the administration.

The EMBS Blog team reserves the right to review and remove submissions that do not meet these standards.

5. Academic Integrity

Students must uphold the principles of academic integrity when using the EMBS Blog:

  • Do not share confidential academic information, such as exam questions or answers.
  • Properly cite any references or sources used in your blog posts.
  • Avoid submitting content that violates the college’s academic policies.

6. Privacy and Confidentiality

Respect the privacy of others when posting on the EMBS Blog:

  • Do not share personal information about other students, faculty, or staff without their consent.
  • Avoid posting sensitive college-related information, such as internal memos or private events.

7. Consequences of Violations

Violations of these community rules may result in:

  • Removal of the offending content from the blog.
  • Temporary or permanent suspension from submitting blogs.
  • Referral to the college administration for further disciplinary action.
